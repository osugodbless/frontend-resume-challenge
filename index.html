TripPlanner App Design

Under the guidance of ALX's experts, our team focused on developing a solution for the problem of ineffective transportation associated with rapid urbanization. We conducted some additional research on this problem and also created some visualizations of data that illustrates the problem, its impact, and its projected trend. Through our brainstorming and ideation session as a team, we came up with the viable solution of developing an app that helps commuters plan and schedule their daily trips. We believed this would decrease travel time and as a result also reduce operational cost for transport companies. Together, we carried out extensive research and worked to create wireframes for the TripPlanner app. Lastly, we created a video showcasing our work and made a pitch for our proposed next steps of development

As the team's UX Researcher and UI/UX Designer, I carefully designed questionnaires for user interviews aimed at understanding user behaviors, needs and preferences. I gathered insights and designed the user interface to be aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly, while also considering the user experience to ensure seamless interactions and intuitive navigation
